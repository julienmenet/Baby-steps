The Monty Hall Problem





The rules of the game

  • The host must always open a door that was not picked by the contestant
  • The host must always open a door to reveal a goat and never the car.
  • The host must always offer the chance to switch between the originally chosen door and the remaining closed door.

Let's say our contestant selects door #1

He must always accept to switch!

Here is why:

Behind door 1 Behind door 2 Behind door 3 Result if staying at door #1 Result if switching to the door offered

Goat Car Goat Goat!! Wins the car!
Goat Goat Car Goat!! Wins the car!
